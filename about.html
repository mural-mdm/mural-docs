Mural Master Data Management 2.0

Mural Master Data Management 2.0 is the upgrade of former Sun Microsystem open source Mural Master Data Management. It is compatible with Oracle Health care Master Person Index 1.0 release. And it is also known as NetGen Master Person Data Management open source community edition Mosaic 2.0. It is developed and contributed to the open source community by NetGen Software.

Key Features

Mural 2.0 platform is built on the top of the Sun Microsystems open source Mural Master Data Management. And it includes the complete components and full capability of its precede.

Additionally the Mural 2.0 adds the following enhancements:

Platform Refresh

● NetBeans 7.0.1

● GlassFish 3.1.1

● Spring Framework 3.0.5

There are significant differences between Spring Framework 2.x and 3.x Upgrading to the Spring Framework 3.0.5 is one of major upgrades.


Multi-Domain Relationship Management

Mural 2.0 contains Multi-Domain Relationship Management plug-in module. The multi-domain relationship management provides the capability of establishing and maintaining relationships within and across desperate master data domains. The mural 2.0 supports Multi-domain design time wizard and runtime multi-domain relationship management service.


Netbeans Project Profiles

NetBeans projects files of the major source modules are checked in for making development and debug experience easier for ever.
